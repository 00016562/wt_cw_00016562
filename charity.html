Charity

Oqtepa Lavash prepared a record 50-meter tandoor-lavash as part of a charity event.

On December 13, 2020, a charity event took place in Tashkent city: setting a record for preparing 50-meter tandoor lavash. All proceeds were sent to the Mehrli Q'ollar charity foundation.

More than 1,500 guests attended the large-scale event. And those who could not share this joyful moment watched with delight what was happening live on the official OqTepa Lavash page on Instagram.
